# Sangathan Onboarding & Training Manual

## 1. 10-Minute Quickstart Guide
**For:** New Organisation Administrators
**Goal:** Achieve operational readiness in 10 minutes.

**Step 1: Verify Email**
*   Sign up with your primary email.
*   Click the verification link sent to your inbox immediately.

**Step 2: Verify Phone (Mandatory)**
*   Enter your mobile number when prompted.
*   Input the OTP received via SMS.
*   *Note:* You cannot access the dashboard without this step.

**Step 3: Add 3 Core Members**
*   Navigate to **Members > Add Member**.
*   Add yourself and two other key stakeholders (e.g., Treasurer, Secretary).
*   Assign appropriate roles (Admin/Editor).

**Step 4: Create First Form**
*   Navigate to **Forms > New Form**.
*   Title it "Membership Intake" or "General Feedback".
*   Add three fields: Name, Phone, Email.
*   Publish and copy the public link.

**Step 5: Schedule First Meeting**
*   Navigate to **Meetings > Schedule**.
*   Set title "Core Team Kickoff".
*   Select the 3 members you added in Step 3.
*   Save the meeting.

**Step 6: Review Donation Settings**
*   Navigate to **Donations**.
*   Familiarize yourself with the "Log Donation" screen.
*   *Note:* No bank account connection is required; this is a ledger.

**Step 7: Export Test File**
*   Navigate to **Settings > Data Export**.
*   Click "Export All Data".
*   Verify the downloaded CSV file opens correctly on your device.

## 2. Admin Onboarding Checklist
**For:** Organisation Owners & Admins

**Security Setup**
- [ ] Primary Admin phone verified.
- [ ] Strong password set (min 12 chars).
- [ ] Unused sessions logged out.

**Role Assignment**
- [ ] Identify 1-2 trusted Editors (limit full Admin access).
- [ ] Verify Editor identities offline before adding.

**Data Hygiene**
- [ ] Define naming convention (e.g., "First Last" vs "Last, First").
- [ ] Check for duplicate phone numbers in member list.

**Transparency & Compliance**
- [ ] Read the Acceptable Use Policy.
- [ ] Create an internal schedule for data exports (e.g., Weekly).
- [ ] Brief core team on "Soft Delete" vs "Permanent Delete".

## 3. Editor Onboarding Guide
**For:** Volunteers or Staff managing daily operations

**What You Can Do**
*   **Add/Edit Members:** Keep the registry updated.
*   **Create Forms:** Launch surveys and petitions.
*   **Log Donations:** Record incoming funds (Cash/UPI).
*   **Mark Attendance:** Record who came to meetings.

**What You Cannot Do**
*   **Delete Organisation:** Only Admins can do this.
*   **Access Billing:** Only Admins manage subscriptions.
*   **Export Full Database:** Restricted to Admins for security.

**Best Practices**
*   **Accuracy First:** Double-check phone numbers before saving.
*   **Verification:** Only mark donations as "Verified" after seeing the bank SMS or cash in hand.
*   **Privacy:** Do not share member lists on external WhatsApp groups.

---

## 4. Viewer Onboarding Guide
**For:** Auditors, Observers, or Senior Advisors

**Role Overview**
*   You have **Read-Only** access.
*   You can see reports, member lists, and logs.
*   You cannot change, delete, or add any data.

**Data Confidentiality**
*   You are a trusted observer.
*   Do not screenshot member lists.
*   Do not share financial summaries outside the organisation without permission.

## 5. First 7-Day Implementation Plan
**Goal:** Transition from "Setup" to "Routine Operation".

**Day 1: Setup**
*   Complete Quickstart Guide.
*   Verify Admin phone.
*   Add core team members.

**Day 2: Member Entry**
*   Import existing members (manual entry or CSV).
*   Clean up duplicate entries.
*   Assign "Active" status.

**Day 3: Forms**
*   Create a "Membership Form" for new signups.
*   Share link on organisation social media/WhatsApp.

**Day 4: Meetings**
*   Schedule the weekly/monthly general body meeting.
*   Test the Jitsi video link.

**Day 5: Donation Log**
*   Backfill recent donations from the last month.
*   Verify totals match your bank statement.

**Day 6: Audit Review**
*   Check **Settings > Audit Logs**.
*   Review actions taken by Editors to ensure accuracy.

**Day 7: Export & Review**
*   Perform full data export.
*   Hold a 15-min review call with core team to discuss any issues.

## 6. Minimum Viable Organisation Structure
**For:** New collectives starting from scratch.

**Roles**
*   **1 Admin (The Convenor):** Owns the account, manages billing/exports.
*   **2 Editors (Treasurer & Secretary):** Manage donations and meeting minutes.
*   **Viewers (Advisory Board):** Oversight only.

**Cadence**
*   **Meetings:** Weekly core team sync (recorded in app).
*   **Donations:** Logged within 24 hours of receipt.
*   **Exports:** Monthly backup stored on secure offline drive.

**Naming Convention**
*   **Members:** `[First Name] [Last Name]` (e.g., "Rahul Kumar").
*   **Donations:** `[Donor Name] - [Month]` (e.g., "Rahul Kumar - Feb 2026").

## 7. Internal Policy Template
**For:** Organisations to adopt as their own rulebook.

**1. Data Policy**
*   Member data collected on Sangathan is solely for organisation use.
*   No member data will be shared with third-party political parties or advertisers.

**2. Fund Transparency**
*   All donations (Cash/UPI) must be logged on Sangathan within 48 hours.
*   Unverified donations will be investigated by the Treasurer weekly.

**3. Admin Accountability**
*   The Admin is responsible for the security of the account.
*   Password sharing is strictly prohibited.
*   Access will be revoked immediately for members who leave the organisation.

**4. Conflict Resolution**
*   In case of dispute, the Audit Log on Sangathan serves as the single source of truth for actions taken.

## 8. Volunteer Onboarding Template
**Process for adding new volunteers:**

1.  **Add to Registry:** Admin adds volunteer as a "Member" with "Active" status.
2.  **Assign Role:** If they need to manage data, upgrade to "Editor". If not, keep as "Member" (no dashboard access).
3.  **Briefing:** Explain the **Internal Policy** (Section 7).
4.  **Probation:** Monitor their Audit Log activity for the first week.
